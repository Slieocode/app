What Is Fasting

Intermittent fasting (IF) or intermittent energy restriction is an umbrella term for various meals timing schedules that cycle between a period of voluntary fasting (or reduced calorie intake) and non-fasting over a defined period.

Fasting Is NOT A Diet

It is a way to live a better, healthier life.

It’s not a temporary diet, it’s a lifestyle
It’s easy, because we don’t limit what you eat, but only when
It’s effective: unlike diets, it does not have a “yo-yo” effect (regaining weight after you stop dieting)
It’s healthy: fasting is the foundation of a healthy lifestyle and you don’t have to limit macro nutrients that are vital to your body
It’s social friendly, because it forgives errors and slips. If you have to eat with your friends - you can do it.
